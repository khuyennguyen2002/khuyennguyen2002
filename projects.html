As a SMASH scholar, I'm currently working amongst my peers on how to reduce the number of adolescents between the ages of 18-22 who get infected by HIV in the United States. We have came up with digital medias such as a website and an Instagram account to spread awareness to teenagers about the disease. As the current generations heavily depend on digital media, having our resoures on a digital platform will be effective in conveying our goals.
